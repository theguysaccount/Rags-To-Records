WolfByte Beats

Dubstep Inspired EDM

Wolfbyte is a duo composed of producers Ryan Enos and Esteban Munoz. Both started dabbling in mixing as early as the rise of skrillex. When they met freshman year of college they started producing music together. They quickly realized their production skills were not like your average college DJ's and have since been booking gigs and gaining a larger and larger following. Their music is indiscernable from top Riddim DJ's trending today and they are simply on the climb of exposure.

Edit by Marc

A local tucson videographer sent in this sweet edit he made from filming of one Wolfbytes first shows.

The future ...

Once they realized they had a future in the EDM scene Ryan and Esteban have made plans to attend Icon Collective Music Production School to master truly their craft.

Abous Us

A management company

Rags to Records is an exclusive artist management company. We believe in quality instead of quantity because each DJ we sign we believe has the potential to break records and top charts. We employ a graphic designer for our artists artwork, a light FX designer for our stage video, and an audio engineer for perfectly mastered beats.

Quote From The Founder

"I started Rags to Records right after I dropped out of college. Wolfbyte was the first DJ's I signed, at first it was unofficial. I simply started using my knowledge of computers and networking to get their name out there. Eventually money started rolling in and we figured it was time to become professional about it. I am a computer nerd at heart, luckily this is the age of information and there is plenty of talent out there that is simply lacking exposure. That is my job is to find it, then to expose it."

Moving Forward...

Rags To Records will be breaking artists out into the EDM scene. As more DJ's are signed on, full sets and events will be hosted by Rags To Records to promote the DJ's we have and to bring in a profit for the venues who host us. We will always act by win, win situations where everyone is happy at the end of the day.
